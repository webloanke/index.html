Terms & Conditions

1. Introduction

These Terms govern the lending relationship between Haraka Loans ("Lender") and the individual borrower ("Borrower"). By creating an account you agree to be bound by this Agreement, governed by the laws of Kenya, including the Microfinance Act, the Central Bank of Kenya Act, and the Data Protection Act 2019.

2. Eligibility

  • Kenyan citizen aged 18 years or older.
  • Valid Kenyan National Identity Card.
  • Active Safaricom M-Pesa registered mobile number.
  • Truthful and accurate information during registration.

3. Loan Products

Unsecured mobile loans from KSh 1,000 to KSh 20,000 in KSh 1,000 increments, subject to credit assessment.

4. Service Fee

A non-refundable 5% service fee is charged at application and collected via the payment link before disbursement.

5. Interest Rate & Repayment

6% monthly interest on outstanding principal, repayable within 6 months. No early repayment penalty. Late repayment may result in CRB listing.

6. Disbursement

Funds disbursed to M-Pesa within 24 hours, normally within 1 hour. Contact us for delays beyond 24 hours.

7. Referral Programme

Users who refer 10 friends are eligible for a free loan (no service fee) applied within 48 hours of their last application. Referrals are tracked by shares made through the in-app referral feature. Haraka Loans reserves the right to verify referrals and withdraw the benefit in case of abuse.

8. Default & Collections

  • Late fees up to 5% of outstanding balance per month.
  • Reporting to licensed Credit Reference Bureaus in Kenya.
  • Legal remedies under Kenyan law.

9. Data Privacy

Personal data handled per Kenya Data Protection Act 2019. Not sold to third parties. Contact harakaloans@proton.me to request data access or deletion.

10. Amendments

Haraka Loans may amend these terms at any time with notice via your registered phone or email.

11. Dispute Resolution

Disputes resolved amicably first; if unresolved within 30 days, referred to arbitration in Nairobi under the Arbitration Act (Cap 49).
